Lavish Instant Noodles

Ingredients:

Ramen 1 package
Eggs 1
Green onion 1/2 stalk, thinkly sliced
Sesame oil Drizzle
Nori 2-3 sheets
  1. Place egg in a medium pot with 3 cups of water and bring to a boil. Cook for about 6 minutes then remove egg
  2. Bring remaining water back up to a boil. Add noodles, flavor packet, and sesame oil.
  3. Cook for 3-4 minutes. Sitr occasionally.
  4. Once done, pour contents in bowl. Cut egg in half and gently place on top of noodles.
  5. Garnish with nori and green onion.
  6. Serve immediately.

Smackin Grilled Cheese

Ingredients:

Bread of choice 2 slices
Cheese of choice 2-3 slices
Butter 2 tspns
  1. Preheat skillet on medium heat
  2. Take a knife and spread butter onto one side of both slices of bread, making sure no spot is uncovered
  3. Place one slice onto skillet, butter side down
  4. Place 3-5 slices of cheese onto bread
  5. Place the other slice of bread on top, butter side up
  6. Flip sandiwch once the bottom slice of bread turns golden brown
  7. Continue cooking till golden brown and cheese is melted
  8. Take off of skillet and cut it diagnally in half
  9. Serve immediately.

Greatest Cereal Ever

Ingredients:

Corn Pops 1.5 cup
Peanut Butter Crunch 1.5 cup
2% Milk 3 cups
  1. Pour Corn Pops into large bowl
  2. Pour Peanut Butter Crunch into same bowl
  3. Carefully pour milk into bowl
  4. Gently stir and mix the cereal together for 5 seconds
  5. Serve immediately.
